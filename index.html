ABOUT ME

Born in the west of Ireland, I grew up in the quiet countryside before pursuing a career in hairdressing. I worked as a hairdresser until I was 25 before taking a career break to diversify my skillset. In the four following years: I moved to west Germany and to south Ireland before settling in Berlin while working at a bank, music festival, hotel restaurant, an airline, a customer service website and a cat shelter. I returned to the hairdressing industry to work as an educator for Toni&Guy in Berlin before the Corona pandemic shook the world. I now continue to have fun with photoshoots in my free time while working as a Web Developer!
